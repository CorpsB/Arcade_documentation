Implements the Pinky class, which inherits directly from the Ghost class. Pinky is a pink ghost. In chase mode, it moves to the player's +4 position (in the player's direction). In scatter mode, she moves to the upper left of the maze. She starts in the cage, but exits directly at the start of the game.
